# IAM Onboarding Guide

New team members get baseline access through the Identity portal during onboarding. Most
data and tooling access is then requested as named entitlements (for example, DL-Reader
for the data lake) and approved by your manager.

## Onboarding checklist

1. Activate SSO and enroll in MFA.
2. Join your team's access group in the Identity portal.
3. Request role-specific entitlements (e.g. DL-Reader) with a business justification.
4. Your manager approves; provisioning is automatic once approved.

For access to the data lake specifically, follow the data-lake access runbook.
